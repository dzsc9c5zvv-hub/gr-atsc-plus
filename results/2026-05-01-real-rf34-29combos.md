## Findings

**Champion: `fpll_a002_tau20`** — FPLL alpha=0.002, AFC tau=20µs, otherwise
stock pipeline. +30 HD frames vs stock (+2.8%), +17 SD frames (+1.6%),
+1.4 pp RS-clean. Slower-than-tight FPLL loop tracks real-world phase
noise more cleanly than alpha=0.003 (last night's recommendation).

**Tau=40µs kills the FPLL.** Three combos (`fpll_a003_tau40`,
`fpll_a002_tau40`, `fpll_soft_a002_tau40`) all hit 0.0% RS-clean. The
AFC time-constant must stay between 10-20µs for the loop to track.

**alpha=0.005 is too aggressive (37%).** Confirmed `very_tight_fpll`
finding from earlier; alpha=0.005 drops 1083 HD → 671 HD on real RF.

**All 7 atsc_equalizer_long combos still at 0.3%** even after the
adaptN_dd → filterN fix and NPRETAPS 0.8 → 0.2. The deeper bug remains
unidentified. Avoid this block until it's debugged further.

**Sync-tunable variants without tight FPLL = no-ops.** `sync_lock2_unlock1`,
`sync_lock4_unlock4`, `hysteresis_sync`, `force_emit_sync`, `max_relax_sync`
all tie stock at 1083 HD / 1044 SD / 60.8%. The sync block isn't the
bottleneck when carrier is locked.

## Recommended capture+decode recipe (post-2026-05-01)

```
rx_sdr.exe -d "driver=sdrplay" -a "Antenna A" -f 593000000 -s 8000000 \
           -g "IFGR=59" -t "rfgain_sel=5" -F CS16 -n 480000000 capture.cs16
python3 run_combo.py capture.cs16 out.ts fpll_a002_tau20
python3 ts_tei_scrub.py out.ts out_clean.ts
vlc out_clean.ts
```

Expect ~1100 HD frames per 60 seconds (~18 fps) of watchable video on
the station's HD primary. SD subchannels render cleanly at the same combo.
