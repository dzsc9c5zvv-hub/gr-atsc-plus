## Findings

**Use `tight_fpll_soft_vit`** — small but consistent win (+1.3pp). Stacks
the tight-FPLL gain (alpha=0.003, AFC τ=20µs) with soft-decision Viterbi.

**`atsc_equalizer_long` is broken on real signal.** Every combo routing
through the 256-tap equalizer flatlines at 0.3% — well below stock's
60.8%. The synthetic-bench delta-init fix did not actually solve the
underlying issue; this is a real regression that needs investigation
or revert.

**`very_tight_fpll` (alpha=0.005) is too aggressive** for real RF phase
noise — drops to 37%. Stick with `tight_fpll` (0.003).

**Sync tunable variants are no-ops** on RF 34 — hysteresis, force-emit,
and max-relax all tie stock at 60.8%. The wall isn't in the sync block
when the signal is already locked.

## Why this differs from the synthetic 2026-04-30 baseline

The earlier synthetic-bench scoreboard claimed the equalizer-long combos
hit 99% RS-clean. That result is unreproducible — both cloud and local
WSL environments now produce 0% on synthetic IQ at SNR=25 dB. The
synthetic generator has an issue that masks downstream block behavior.
**Real-IQ benchmarking against a known capture is the new source of
truth** until the synth is fixed.
